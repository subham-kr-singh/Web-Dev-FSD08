Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cravings collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our services. By using Cravings, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

  • Account information and profile data
  • Order history and preferences
  • Device and usage data
  • Precise location data (when enabled)

  • To process your orders seamlessly
  • To personalise your platform experience
  • To send related updates and promotions
  • To improve platform security and functionality

We only share essential information with:

  • Restaurants fulfilling your order
  • Riders delivering your order
  • Payment processors for transactions
  • Law enforcement only when required by law

We use essential and analytics cookies to improve your experience. You can opt-out using your browser settings.

We retain your active account data, keep order history for 5 years for tax reasons, and anonmyze specific analytic data long-term.

You have the right to access, correct, delete, port your data, or opt-out of marketing communications.
