OUR STORY

Born from a love of great food

Started in 2022, Cravings was born out of a simple idea: everyone deserves access to great food, anywhere, anytime. We began in a small neighborhood and have rapidly grown into a city-wide favorite.

Our platform is designed to seamlessly connect local restaurants with food lovers, while empowering our dedicated delivery partners. We believe in creating a community where businesses thrive and customers are always satisfied.
